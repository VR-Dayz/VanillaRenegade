Rules

These rules apply in game and in Discord where relevant.

1. Discord conduct rules

1.0 No family insults
Insulting relatives (mothers, fathers, siblings, etc.) is forbidden. Everyone has family. Keep it respectful.

1.1 No hate speech or politics
Do not incite ethnic or racial hatred. Do not discuss political or religious topics. Do not promote Nazism, fascism, or similar ideologies.

1.2 Respectful language
Unreasonable insults toward community members are forbidden. Senseless swearing in Discord channels is not allowed.

1.3 No meta gaming in Discord
Do not post in game information. Examples: gunshots at NWAF, car at Zeleno, Tisy active.

1.4 No base or stash locations
Never post base or stash locations. This applies even if the locations belong to you.

1.5 Meeting up with friends
Use Discord DMs or available voice channels. Do not post meet up requests in public text channels.

2. General rules

2.0 Respect the staff
Treat staff with respect at all times.

2.1 No stream sniping
Watching streams to gain in game advantages is forbidden.

2.2 Group size limit
Maximum of 6 players online at the same time. Larger groups may exist, but only 6 can be online simultaneously.

2.3 No racism or public attacks
Racist remarks, hate speech, or public harassment are strictly prohibited.

2.4 No doxxing or personal information
Do not share personal details of other players. Includes Steam, Discord, or other linked account info.

2.5 No server promotion
Promotion or mentioning of other servers or projects is forbidden.

2.6 No political or religious topics
These discussions are not allowed in game or in Discord.

2.7 No admin impersonation or misinformation
Do not pretend to be staff. Do not spread false information about rules or server operation.

2.8 No cheats or unfair software
Cheats, hacks, or scripts are forbidden. Allowed: Nvidia filters, crosshair tools.

2.9 No exploiting bugs or vulnerabilities
Do not exploit game or server vulnerabilities. If you find a bug, report it in the bug report channel.

2.10 No circumventing group size
Any attempt to bypass group size restrictions will result in a ban.

2.11 No hiding items in textures
Exploiting bugs to hide loot in textures is forbidden.

2.12 Staff authority
Staff may ban without warning. Staff are not obliged to provide evidence.

2.13 Rule changes
Rules may be changed at any time at the discretion of Vanilla Renegade, particularly to address grey areas or situations not yet considered.

2.14 Alt accounts
Each player may use only one account. Any alternate accounts discovered will result in a permanent ban of the alt account and a ban of up to one month on the primary account. If more than one player connects from the same IP address, a support ticket must be submitted in advance with an explanation. Both players will be required to log into the server and Discord simultaneously with an administrator and complete verification tasks to confirm each account is controlled by a different individual. Failure to disclose or report alternate accounts will result in a ban.

3. Base building rules

3.0 Flag requirement
Every base must have a flag placed within 5 days. The flag will protect 50m in all directions of your base.

3.1 Flag time limit
Bases without a flag after 5 days will be opened by admins for public looting.

3.2 No coastal building
Building in coastal areas (see map) is forbidden. Punishment: base will be deleted without warning.

3.3 Restricted areas
No building in police stations, military zones, or medical zones. Bases must be at least 200m away from military zones.

3.4 Door limit
Maximum of 15 locked doors per base. Garage doors max 1. Doors used only as windows with no loot behind do not count toward the limit.

3.5 Movement space
Leave space between walls to allow free movement.

3.6 No overlapping or clipping structures
Walls, frames, and doors must not overlap, clip into, or pass through any existing map structures. All doors must be fully free from and not intersect with any map assets. Admin will remove illegal doors.

3.7 One base per group
Each group may only have 1 base. A garage is allowed if close to the base but limited to 1 locked door.

3.8 Code locks not to be shared
Code locks are not to be shared with other groups. Any member letting themselves into another base other than their own will receive up to a month ban. Sharing codes with other groups will be treated as base sharing and both parties will be punished.

3.9 Accessible loot
All loot must be accessible and raidable through legitimate gameplay mechanics. Loot may not be hidden behind unraidable walls.

3.10 Watch tower limits
Maximum height of two full watch towers.

3.11 Tents
Tents are not to be stacked inside each other.

4. Event rules

4.0 Respect the admin
Follow instructions from the event admin at all times. Disrespect or ignoring commands may result in removal from the event and being sent back to the coast.

4.1 Behaviour during events
Do not trash talk or fight other participants before the event starts. Do not bring personal weapons or gear unless explicitly allowed. Keep chat to a minimum so admins can communicate instructions clearly.

4.2 Event participation
Most events provide teleportation and gear. Stay away from event zones until the event officially begins.

4.3 Death during events
If killed during an event, you will not be teleported back to base. Winners may receive rewards and teleport back to base.

5. Raid rules

5.0 Raid times
Raids are only allowed on weekends. Start Friday 16:00 CET. End Sunday 23:59 CET.

5.1 Gate only raiding
Raids must be done through gates only. Walls, watchtowers, or other structures cannot be destroyed.

5.2 No base takeovers
You may not claim, recode, or move into a raided base. Take what you want and leave.

5.3 No glitching
Entering bases by glitching is forbidden. Entering via windows is allowed. Punishment: ban.

5.4 Recording recommended
Record when you start raiding, from beginning to end. Without video evidence, disputes will favour the base owner.

5.5 Damage registration issues
If explosives or ammo damage does not register, loot will only be restored with video proof.

5.6 No logging out in raids
Do not log out inside an enemy base during or after a raid.

5.7 No allied raiding
Raids must only involve your own group. No allied groups may raid, patrol, or PvP together during a raid.

5.8 Obscure entry
If you use an unusual entry method, you must explain it to staff if asked. Screenshots or videos help clarify.

5.9 Boosting rules
You may boost using 1 placed object at a time (player, tent, vehicle). You may not stack placed objects. Map structures do not count as placed objects.

5.10 Logging into foreign bases
If you log into a foreign base, you must suicide. Do not dismantle back out.

5.11 No dismantling
Dismantling bases is forbidden.

5.12 No loot destruction
Do not ruin, despawn, or burn loot. If you cannot carry it, leave it. No dropping loot on the ground.

5.13 Raid cooldown 48 hours
A raid is valid if attackers gain access to at least one loot room. If attackers enter a loot room but defenders repel the raid and seal the base, the raid is defended and the same group may not raid that base again for 48 hours. If attackers fail to enter any loot room, the attempt is not considered a raid and multiple attempts are permitted. If a base has been fully raided and remains open, returning for additional loot runs is not considered double raiding.

6. Vehicle rules

6.0 Vehicle limit
Each team may secure a maximum of 2 vehicles. Any extra vehicles must be left outside the base with all parts attached so others can take them. Do not store wheels and components inside your base.

6.1 No refunds for bugged vehicles
Vehicles lost underground due to map exploits or driving errors (for example driving outside of the map) will not be refunded.

6.2 Vehicles are not protected
Vehicles can be looted or destroyed by anyone. It is up to you to secure your vehicle. No compensation will be provided for lost vehicles or items stored inside them.

7. Chat and community rules

7.0 Language
English only in global chat and Discord. Voice channels may use other languages.

7.1 No staff impersonation
Do not pretend to be staff in any form.

7.2 No meta gaming in chat
Do not give away your or others’ in game locations in side chat.

7.3 No spamming
Spamming global chat will result in a mute or kick.

7.4 Respect staff
Disrespectful or inappropriate comments towards staff will lead to punishment.

7.5 Tagging rules
Only tag Discord roles in emergencies (for example all servers down). Do not tag staff for ticket responses.

7.6 No public cheating accusations
Do not accuse players of cheating in public chat or private messages. Submit a ticket instead.

7.7 Community reputation
Staff may remove players if their public profiles contain content that reflects poorly on the community.

8. Ticket and support rules

8.0 No tagging staff
Do not tag staff if your ticket is not answered immediately. Tickets will be handled when staff are available.

8.1 Final say
Admins have the final decision in all tickets and disputes.

8.2 Honesty required
Do not lie or provide false information in tickets. This includes false reports and compensation requests.

8.3 Recording recommended
Use recording software (for example MedalTV, Nvidia) since most tickets require video proof.

8.4 Provide proof and details
Include as much detail and evidence as possible. Include your in game name in the ticket.

8.5 No DMing admins
Do not private message admins unless asked. Use the ticket system for all issues.

8.6 No teleport requests
Do not ask admins for teleports. Submit a ticket if you encounter an issue.

8.7 Submit evidence quickly
Upload videos to YouTube as unlisted and include the link in your ticket.

9. Fair play rules

9.0 VAC ban policy
Any account with a VAC ban less than 365 days old will be permanently banned. No exceptions.

9.1 Zero tolerance for cheating
Cheats, exploits, or unfair advantages are forbidden. Play with respect, fairness, and integrity.

9.2 Abuse of game mechanics
Exploiting unintended mechanics for unfair advantage is forbidden. Examples include placing objects inside walls, logging off inside bunkers, using indestructible objects as shields, and anything deemed unethical or not intended by the game developers.

9.3 No ghosting
Abusing log in or log out mechanics to gain advantage is forbidden. Examples include logging out inside enemy bases to bypass defences and logging in behind enemy lines after allies start combat.

9.4 Reporting suspected cheaters
Do not call out suspected cheaters in chat. Submit a ticket so staff can investigate covertly.

9.5 Multiple bans
Having multiple public bans linked to your account may result in a ban from this server.

10. Combat log rules

10.0 You are considered in combat if
You shoot or are shot at. You are trapped inside your base during a raid.

10.1 Logout timer
You may not log out until 5 minutes after your last point of contact. This is enforced by a logout timer.

10.2 Logging out during raids
Logging out with loot or storage items during a raid is combat logging.

10.3 Forced combat logout
If you force a combat logout, your character will be killed.

11. Compensation rules

11.0 Video evidence required
Video proof is mandatory for all compensation requests. Without evidence, staff may deny your claim.

11.1 No compensation for
Items dropped on the ground.
Items that despawn due to lifetime expiry.
Items lost on server restart.
Vehicles or loot inside vehicles unless explicitly approved by an admin.

11.2 Recording software
Use recording software (for example MedalTV, Nvidia). Set it up to record the previous minutes so you have proof when needed. No recording means no support.
